OUR PURPOSE

Forecasted breaks the weather down for you so you don't have to. We provide accurate and simple weather forecasts for the busybodies in life. Our goal is to make lives easier by quickly telling you what to expect and how you can easily prepare for it in your daily life.

OUR DOMAIN

OUR LOGO

The Forecasted logo features a thermometer with a sun as the base. Our simple visuals align with our simple weather philosophy.
