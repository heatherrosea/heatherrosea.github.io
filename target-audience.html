TARGET AUDIENCE

The target audience is 25-35 year-old adults who work full-time jobs and have 1-2 children. They have a college education and have an average salary of about $60,000. They live in larger cities around the United States.

PERSONA ONE: DAVID

David is a 32 year-old male who lives in Seattle. He works in HR at a local tech company. He is married and expecting his first child.

SCENARIOS:

David has to commute to work and needs to know whether his car will make it on the roads or not due to the weather.

Solution: We offer the most up-to-date forecast for the morning, and we mark if the forecast could indicate dangerous roads.

David's favorite hobby is camping, but he barely has time for it. When he plans a camping trip, he needs to know it will be worth his time.

Solution: We provide forecasts for more rural areas and can show as many days ahead as the user needs.

PERSONA TWO: JULIE

Julie is a 28 year-old female who lives in Salt Lake City. She runs her own successful photography business and had her first child six months ago. She took a break from work, but is ready to start booking clients again.

SCENARIOS:

Julie needs to know if the weather is suitable for an outdoor photograph session. Unexpected bad weather is not an option as it would ruin her shoot.

Solution: We keep our forecast information updated constantly so there aren't any second guesses.

Julie is a cautious first-time mom and worries about her child in the winter. She needs to know how cold it will get so she can dress her baby appropriately.

Solution: We provide information along with temperature, such as wind chill and estimated snowfall.

Julie often visits family in neighboring cities and wants to easily access between cities.

Solution: We allow users to favorite certain cities or areas and group cities by areas.
